I am with the [Human Communication Technologies Lab](http://hct.ece.ubc.ca) supervised by [Prof. Sidney Fels](http://hct.ece.ubc.ca/person/sid-fels/) and [Dr. Gregor Miller](http://www.ece.ubc.ca/~gregor/).
